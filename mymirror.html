My M*rror Game

Description

Who am I? I don't know who I am or what I look like. Why can't I see myself in the mirror? Why is it like this? What is wrong with me?

Game Outline

Initial Planning:

  • Core idea:

    • This game will be a sort of dark psychological action adventure type of game. The player begin playing as a character that doesn't know what they look like. The premise of the game is the character can't see what they look like but wants to find out who they are. When the player passes through levels, small parts of the character will be revealed.

  • Target audience:

    • This game is meant to be played by everyone. Should appeal to multiple audiences.
