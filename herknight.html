Her Knight Game

Description

I don't need to be saved from this tower. Fuck you for thinking that! I can write my own story. I will do what I need to do. I will do what I want. I will love who I want.

Game Outline

Initial Planning:

  • Core idea:

    • This is an action adventure game centering around a queer wlw love story between a princess that doesn't want to be saved and a friend she meets that wanted to save her. The player will play as the friend that wanted to save the princess.

  • Target audience:

    • Targeted towards queer women and nonbinary people. For people who enjoy wlw, nblw, and nblnb.
