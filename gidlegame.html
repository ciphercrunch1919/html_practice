The Heart of Neverland [(G)I-DLE Game]

Description

You are a neverland? Then enter this au game and play as one of your favorite members of (G)I-DLE. Solve new mysteries, play trivial games, and decorate your dorm room and get to know the rest of the members.

Game Outline

Initial Planning:

  • Core idea:

    • This will be a sims like arcade game that centers around the player picking one of the members of (G)I-DLE to play. The player needs to have an account for this game and will be able to have multiple storylines to follow. The player should be able to play any member they want to.
